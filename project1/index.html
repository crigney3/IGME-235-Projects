Eat N' Shoot is a physics based browser game that acts like a combination of the cell stage from Spore and a classic Shoot 'Em Up like Asteroids.

The player navigates around an environment, looking to eat things and grow bigger! The bigger you get, the better the food, and the bigger the threats. At the same time, keep yourself safe with your projectile weapon!
